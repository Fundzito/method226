The 226 Method

The 226 Method is about using fitness as a path to self-realization. Discipline in the gym becomes discipline in life. This is your journey to building strength, focus, and consistency in every area of life. We all have a story to share. As the kids say, a comeback arc - this is mine. Stick to the 226 Method, and by the end, it will be time for you to share A Story of Your Own.
