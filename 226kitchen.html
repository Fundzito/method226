The Kitchen - Cooking, Meals & Prep

Master your meals, plan ahead, and make healthy swaps without sacrificing taste or convenience. The 226 Method keeps your kitchen fun and practical.

Smart Meal Swaps

Veggie Swap

Replace fries with roasted veggies, salad, or air-popped popcorn.

Protein Upgrade

Choose grilled chicken, fish, or tofu instead of fried meat.

Pasta Swap

Whole grain pasta with tomato-based sauce instead of creamy sauces.

Smart Drinks

Water, sparkling water, or light wine over sugary beverages.

Snack Swap

Fruit, Greek yogurt, or portioned nuts instead of chips or candy.

ACV Boost

1-2 tsp ACV (raw, unfiltered)

Add-Ins

Turmeric + black pepper, fresh ginger, lemon juice, honey

Instructions

Mix ACV with warm water, add add-ins, stir, drink before meals

226 Pro Tip
Plan meals ahead and prep in batches. Simple swaps and portion control make healthy eating effortless, even on busy days.

The 226 Plate

Vegetables

40-50% of plate
Broccoli, Spinach, Peppers, Green Beans, Mixed Veggies

Protein

30-40% of plate
Chicken, Fish, Eggs, Lean Beef, Turkey, Tofu

Carbs

20-30% of plate
Rice, Potatoes, Pasta, Whole Grain Bread, Quinoa

226 Pro Tip
Keep protein and veggies the main focus. Carbs in moderation give energy without spiking calories. Prepping ahead keeps meals stress-free.
