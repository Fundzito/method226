The Fun Page - Life, Nights Out & Social Eating

Life isn't just workouts and meals. The 226 Method shows you how to enjoy nights out, family dinners, and social events while still staying on track.

Drinking Smart & Staying Mindful

Swap Drinks

Swap mixed drinks for spritzers or shots.

Choose Wisely

Choose wine over dark liquor for fewer calories.

Limit Intake

Max 2 drinks or 4 shots.

Pace Yourself

Alternate drinks with water to stay hydrated.

Be Mindful

It takes ~30 min of exercise to burn 300 calories, but only 30 sec to consume. Choose wisely!

226 Pro Tip
You don't have to avoid alcohol (even though I would recommend you stop completely) or indulgence to stick to the 226 Method. Be intentional, stay hydrated, pace yourself, and remember your long-term goals. Life is for enjoying, not stressing.

Quick Fun Swaps - Eat, Drink, Enjoy

Fries & Chips

Swap for side salad, roasted veggies, or popcorn.

Burger Swap

Bunless burger, grilled chicken sandwich, or veggie wrap.

Pasta Swap

Whole grain pasta with tomato-based sauce or sautéed veggies.

Cocktails

Spritzers, light wine, or shots; alternate with water.

Desserts

Portion-controlled treat, Halo Top, or fruit-based dessert.

Soda Swap

Sparkling water with lemon/lime, unsweetened iced tea.

Appetizers

Share with friends or pick 1 small portion.

The 226 Night Out Plate

Vegetables

40-50% of your plate
Broccoli, Spinach, Asparagus, Peppers, Mixed Veggies

Protein

30-40% of your plate
Chicken, Fish, Eggs, Lean Beef, Turkey, Tofu

Carbs

20-30% of your plate
Rice, Potatoes, Pasta, Whole Grain Bread, Quinoa

226 Pro Tip
Eating out doesn't mean giving up control. Fill most of your plate with protein and veggies, enjoy carbs in moderation, and stick to a portion you can finish comfortably. Keep social meals fun without derailing progress.
